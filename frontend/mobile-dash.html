FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my NetBanking password?

Go to My Profile > Reset Password and enter your new 4-digit passcode.

How do I block a lost debit card?

Currently, you must contact customer support or visit your nearest branch immediately to block a lost card.

What are the daily transfer limits?

The default daily transfer limit across all channels (UPI, IMPS, NEFT) is ₹2,00,000.

Can I open a Fixed Deposit via the app?

Yes! On the Home tab, under 'Suggested for you', tap the Fixed Deposit icon to apply instantly.
